Everything a pilot needs

Built around the real workflows of owner-operators and clubs, not bloated with features you'll never use.

Electronic Logbooks

Separate airframe, engine, and propeller logbooks. Every entry linked to a flight or maintenance action.

Maintenance Triggers

Calendar dates, hours, and cycle thresholds. Smart estimates based on recent usage — no surprises.

Flight Logging

Enter hobbs/tach start & end, attach a hobbs photo as proof, track costs per flight.

Secure & Private

Mandatory 2FA (TOTP), encrypted daily backups, multi-tenant ready. Self-hosted on your own hardware.

fleet status

The per-aircraft dashboard shows color-coded status, current hobbs, last 30-day hours, and a list of upcoming and overdue maintenance items — all on one screen.

  • Cost tracking with L / gal conversion
